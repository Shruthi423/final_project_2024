A Journey Through My Favorite Animated Movies

Why only Animated Movies?

I can still remember the first time I saw the Minions scrambling to save their evil masters, only to make everything hilariously worse. I also remember the first time I saw Scrat in Ice Age, desperately chasing his acorn, no matter how much chaos it caused. And who could forget the breathtaking morning in The Lion King when the African jungle comes alive with such beauty?

These moments have always stayed with me, and they’re the reason why animated movies are so special to me, they make us laugh, feel, and sometimes even teach us something, no matter our age. For me, these films are more than just stories; they’ve shaped my childhood, opened magical doors for creativity, and continue to bring comfort and joy every time I watch them.

This site is my way of celebrating the magic they’ve brought into my life, one frame at a time.
